Our Story

A New Standard
in All-Day Dining

Basko reimagines the all-day culinary bar — where luxury meets laid-back sophistication. Perched atop Starling Mall, our rooftop is a sanctuary that seamlessly transitions from afternoon sunsets to late-night revelry.

Our menu is a gastronomic journey across continents — modern Indian, Turkish, Pan-Asian, and European cuisines converge on a single table. Paired with cocktails crafted by world-class mixologists.
